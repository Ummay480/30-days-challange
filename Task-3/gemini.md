Q1: What new improvements were introduced in Gemini 3.0?

Ans:	The main improvements in Gemini 3.0 (released November 18, 2025):
	Deep Thinking: New, advanced reasoning capabilities that perform at a "PhD-level" on complex, multi-step problems (e.g., science and math).
	Generative UI: The model now creates interactive interfaces (like custom apps or visual layouts) in real-time, rather than just text.
	Agentic Coding: Introduction of the Google Antigravity platform, which allows the AI to autonomously plan, code, execute, and debug full software workflows.
	Vibe Coding: Superior ability to translate abstract or high-level intent (the "vibe") into working, responsive code.
	True Multimodality: World-leading performance in understanding and synthesizing information across text, image, audio, and video within a single session.
	Model Variants: Launch of Gemini 3 Pro (flagship) and the ultra-reasoning mode Gemini 3 Deep Think.

Q3: How does Gemini 3.0 improve multimodal understanding?

Ans: 	Gemini 3.0 (Gemini 3) makes the following major improvements to multimodal understanding:
	World-Leading Synthesis: It is explicitly designed as the "best model in the world for multimodal understanding," seamlessly synthesizing information across text,
	images, video, audio, and code in a single prompt.
	Deep Video Insight: It excels at video reasoning (scoring 87.6% on Video-MMMU), allowing it to analyze and answer complex questions about long-form videos
	(up to 45 minutes) and extract insights from sports performance clips or lectures.
	Enhanced Fidelity: It features a refined cross-modal fusion architecture, enabling higher fidelity and more consistent reasoning over mixed inputs like scientific
	diagrams, PDF charts, and user interface screenshots.
	Higher Resolution Control: Developers gain granular control over vision processing via the media_resolution parameter, balancing detail (e.g., reading fine print)
	with cost and latency.
